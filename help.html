Help

Start by searching your desired ingredient or meal in the search bar. The home page will show random meals and ingredients based on your preferences. Add user preferences by clicking on the user icon in the top right corner.
After submitting your search, look amongst the results for whatever meal or ingredients interest you. You can view the meals' ingredient list and save them to your collection (which can be viewed in the "Saved" page).
